INSTALL
-------

1. Copy the urlicon.module to the Drupal modules/ directory.

2. Go to Administer->Site building->Modules (?q=admin/build/modules), and enable the urlicon.module

3. Go to Administer->Site configuration->Input formats(?q=admin/settings/filters) and enable the filter in the desired input formats.

4. Optionally rearrange the filters on the input format's 'rearrange' tab to resolve conflicts with other filters.

5. You may manually overwrite favicons, by placing new icons in your files/urlicon directory
